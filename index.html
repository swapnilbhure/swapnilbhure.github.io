Bhure Swapnil Shadanan

 

I am a PhD student in Materials Engineering Department at Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.

I am working on multi-scale computational modelling, simulation and experimental study of metal additive manufacturing process in Materials Modelling Group with Prof Abhik Choudhury. The aim of my study is to produce single-crystal CMSX-4 (Ni-based superalloy) parts using additive manufacturing.
